Hello my name is Brittany Chu. Welcome to my website! I am 21 years old. I am from Oakland, California. I graduated at Castro Valley High School, CA. I am studying at the University of California, Riverside. My major is Psychology. My minor is Business Administration under organizational behavior. I am interested in human resource.

I am skilled with Microsoft Office, Adobe Photoshop 7, digital Photography, and Imovie. I am also bilingual in English and Chinese. My hobbies are swimming, drawing, painting, and taking pictures. I also enjoy volunteering at the salvation army during the holidays. Seeing elders or homeless people able to have a Thanksgiving and Christmas meal makes me feel very happy.

I worked at Tj maxx as a cashier for 5 months. Through the job, I learned about customer service, efficiency, and attention to detail. For example, there was this one time where I had to deal with a customer with a check payment. However, her check was not valid. Therefore I had to reject her payment. Because of that, she got angry and wanted to complain to my manager. Also, the cashier line was getting really long. So I had to figure out a solution. First, I called for more back-up cashiers, so they can help other customers. Then, called for my manager while explaining to the customer why her check did not work for the transaction. It was very stressful but from then on, I learned how to deal with stressful situations.

The reason why I wanted to pursue a career in human resources is because I found out that it was my passion to help and guide people. Ever since I was little, I always liked helping others. Since my family could not understand English very well, I translate documents and help them out with daily life things. I realized that I could best achieve my goal through human resources. I am hoping to learn more about Business administration in the upcoming year to better improve my understanding of human resources.
